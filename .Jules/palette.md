## 2025-05-14 - Improve sidebar navigation accessibility and clarity
**Learning:** Icon-only navigation items in a collapsed sidebar can be ambiguous. Providing tooltips on hover ensures users can identify menu items without expanding the sidebar. Additionally, descriptive ARIA labels for toggle buttons improve accessibility for screen readers.
**Action:** Always wrap icon-only buttons or collapsed menu items with Ant Design Tooltips and add descriptive aria-labels to maintain clarity and accessibility.
